What do we do?

 Mission

Fancy yourself as politically active? Fan of shows like the
"Daily Show?" If so, then the New York State Young
Democrats are here for you! Stuy's local chapter, founded
last year, is all about having fun while keeping you
updated on the political climate throughout the city.
We'll be providing plenty of awesome internship
opportunities year-round! We've also had guest speakers
such as mayoral candidates, city council candidates and
political operatives stop by, with even more to come this
year! Join us on Thursdays in room 335 if you want to hear
more, hope to see you all there!
